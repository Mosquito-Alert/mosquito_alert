About Tigatrapp

This project has been made possible thanks to support from multiple public and private entities and the work of numerous individuals.

The project is coordinated by the ICREA Movement Ecology Lab at the Center for Advanced Studies of Blanes, Consejo Superior de Investigaciones Científicas (CEAB-CSIC), and CREAF.

The project is cofinanced by the Spanish Foundation for Science and Technology (FECYT) and the Ministry of Economy and Competitiveness, as well as by the "la Caixa" Foundation, the Recercaixa research support program's “Citizen Science: Education and Research“ program, and lokímica.

All of the photos used in Tigatrapp were provided by R. Eritja.
