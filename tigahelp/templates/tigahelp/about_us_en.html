Coordinating Entities

Mosquito Alert is led by the following public research institutions: the Spanish National Research Council’s Center for Advanced Studies of Blanes (CEAB-CSIC), the Center for Ecological Research and Forestry Applications (CREAF) and Pompeu Fabra University (UPF). The Catalan Institution for Research and Advanced Studies (ICREA) also participates.

Collaborators

Mosquito Alert is made possible also thanks to the participation of a number of experts (professionals who, for example, validate citizen scientists’ data), and entities from various countries working in the field of public health, invasive species control, and environmental education.

Financing and participation in projects

Mosquito Alert participates in competitive projects for research, scientific communication dissemination (including the Spanish Plan Estal, the EU’s Horizon 2020 funding line, and “la Caixa” Foundation projects, among others). Mosquito Alert also relies on contributions of resources from the leading and collaborating entities.

More information on entities, collaborations and projects can be found at www.mosquitoalert.com and on social networks.
